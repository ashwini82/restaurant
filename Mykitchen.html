PALAK PANEER

What is Palak Paneer Palak= spinach (in hindi) Paneer= Indian cottage cheese So, this curry is made with spinach and Indian cottage cheese. The spinach is blanched and then pureed. It’s then cooked with spices and simmered on low heat with paneer. Cream is added to cut down on the bitterness of the spinach leaves and also make it rich. If you don’t want to add cream, you can also use yogurt or milk in place. palak paneer served in a bowl with rice
